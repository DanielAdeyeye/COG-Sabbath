Youth Ministry

Vision

To see youths of the Church of God Sabbath Keeping thriving in their spiritual growth in God,
committed to fostering social relationships with each other, and emboldened to passionately express the message of the kingdom of God.

Mission

To support local youth ministries in their pursuit to foster Christian young person’s spiritual, emotional, and holistic growth in God;
to build local youth groups where they are non existent or temporarily suspended; and to encourage relational fellowship amongst Christian youths of the Church of God Sabbath Keeping ministries.
