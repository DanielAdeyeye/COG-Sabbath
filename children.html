Children's Ministry

Vision

Our children will know who they are in Christ and will be leaders in our
society by upholding the standards of God.

Mission

To reach our communities by meeting the needs of the children.
We are inclined to provide meaningful spiritual, social, intellectual and emotional experiences that will prepare our children for life.
By expressing and teaching the love of God, we will guide children to embrace their identity as children of God.
