Men's Mininstry

Vision

To assist every congregation in developing an effective Men’s Ministry.
Develop Men’s Ministry in all existing and future congregation.
Create a world class Men’s Ministry; that will act as a working model for Men’s Ministry locally and internationally.

Mission

o build men’s faith, relationships and ministry, through resources that we obtain, events we produce and ongoing leadership development.
Create an atmosphere of support, encouragement and prayer for individuals that is driven by men’s ministry.
This Ministry is about men encountering Jesus Christ and being developed in their faith to be all Jesus wants them to be
