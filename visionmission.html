Vision and Mission Statement

Vision

The Church of God Sabbath Keeping builds and mobilizes the body of Christ to enrich the lives of individuals, families and our communities. We impact people of all nations with the saving knowledge of Christ and His coming Kingdom, through the leading of the Holy Spirit and obedience to God’s word.

Mission

The mission of The Church of God Sabbath keeping is, to preach and teach the gospel of Jesus Christ. To demonstrate the love of God by addressing spiritual, social, and economic needs of individuals within the local and international communities. To enhance the development of our members through various forms of training.
